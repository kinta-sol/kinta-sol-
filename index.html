Biografía

Inicios musicales

KINTA SOL,comenzó su viaje artístico en Guinea Ecuatorial, inspirada por la riqueza cultural de su tierra .

Su primer álbum, grabado en Francia, marcó un antes y un después en su carrera como artista independiente.

Segundo álbum y evolución artística

Actualmente trabaja en su segundo proyecto musical, explorando nuevas fusiones entre afro pop, flamenco y sonidos contemporáneos.

Biography

Musical Beginnings

KINTA SOL,She began her artistic journey in Equatorial Guinea, inspired by the cultural richness of her land.

Key Collaborations

She has collaborated with Equatorial Guinean artists such as Jamin Dog Bo, in the song Bosques Verdes, with the rapper Adjoguening in his album Menchu, in the singer's first album she collaborated with the French Medy Custos, strengthening her presence in the African scene.

Rompe Cadenas Album

Her debut album, recorded in France, marked a turning point in her career as an independent artist.

Second Album & Artistic Evolution

She is currently working on her second musical project, blending Afro pop, flamenco, and contemporary sounds.

Cultural & Educational Vision

She promotes cultural values, African identity, and youth empowerment through art and education.
